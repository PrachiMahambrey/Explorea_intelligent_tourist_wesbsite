EXPLOREA brings together globetrotters from diverse backgrounds that share the love of travel. From picturesque beaches to architectural marvels of India. Raising a cheer at the apex of Golconda Fort, waking up to the mesmerizing view of beautiful valleys of Ladakh, ziplining over the lush Rathambore forests, shopping in Delhi's vast Sarojini market and being awestruct learning about the rich cultural history of the forts at Rajasthan are the kind of experiences everybody should get once in their life and we are here to help you with just that!

EXPLOREA is our way of encouraging people to explore this beautiful nation. Explorea's core team has visited most of the states of the country and firmly believes that the world should be seen like a traveller and not a tourist.
